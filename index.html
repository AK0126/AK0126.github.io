About Me

I graduated from Harvard College in 2025 with a concentration in Mathematics and a secondary in Computer Science. Starting fall 2025, I'll be at the University of Cambridge to study at the Part III of the Mathematical Tripos.

Outside of math, I enjoy practicing kendo, a Japanese martial art translating to the "way of the sword." I started kendo as a college first-year, and I'm currently ranked shodan.
