HSYLC 2025

Shenzhen, China

August 8th - 18th, 2025

I had such a blast teaching my second iteration of the seminar Rational Points on Elliptic Curves!! HSYLC (Harvard Summit for Young Leaders in China) is a week-long program where bright high school students from China experience a liberal arts education; these students take a variety of seminars taught by Harvard students. The goal of my seminar was to expose students to abstract college-level mathematics through the study of group theory and elliptic curves.
